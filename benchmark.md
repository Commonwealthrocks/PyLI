## Test 1: (RLE-stuffed 1.67GB file)
Aka file full of repeated data!!

| Tool     | Format | Settings | Final Size |
|----------|--------|----------|------------|
| WinRAR   | RAR    | Best     | **88 KB**  |
| PyLI     | n/a    | Best     | 334 KB     |

**Overall WinRAR wins on repeated stuff, unsurprisingly...**

## Notes
